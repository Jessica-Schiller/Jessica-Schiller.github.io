EHR Integration

Why was this app made?

This project was created in the context of an idea visualization. A new concept for integration into digital patient records was to be presented, for which a visualization of the basic idea was needed. Without concrete content specifications, the following screens were created only with a request for proposal. The important thing was that the facts and the idea were correctly understood and presented.

Requirements

Concrete requirements were not defined, so a user flow was created and the most important aspects were filtered out from the information provided.
