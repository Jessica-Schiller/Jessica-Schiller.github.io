UX/UI Design

Tangible User Interface

Why was this app made?

Development of a tangible user interface in the context of remote work and virtual teams as a contrasting interaction concept to the increasing shift of interaction to the virtual world. The result is an interactive prototype based on a programmed microcontroller and realized by 3D printing.

see prototype

Challenge

Most people listen to music. No matter which genre, music follows us through the day. Often we have playlist we listen in the morning, we want to concentrate or when we finish work. We listen music depending on what we do and in which mood we are. What if our music player would be able to adapt to our current situation?


Solution

As solution a concept for a musicapp was developed. The solution allows the User to categorize his music by mood. The User is able to choose a mood and fitting music will be played.
