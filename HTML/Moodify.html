UX/UI Design

Moodify

Why was this app made?

Moodify is an app that allows to listen to music based on the mood. It was made for a class on mobile interaction design. The goal of this app is to enrich the way we listen to music and make it adapt to our lives.

Role: UX & UI Designer
Tools: Figma, Marvel App
Timeline: 6 weeks
